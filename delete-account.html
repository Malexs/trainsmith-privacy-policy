Account Deletion – TrainSmith

You can delete your TrainSmith account directly within the mobile application.

How to Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your account and all associated personal data:

  1. Open the TrainSmith app on your device.
  2. Go to the Settings section.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Once the deletion is confirmed, your account and associated data will be removed permanently and cannot be recovered.
