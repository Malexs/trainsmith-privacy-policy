1. Introduction

Welcome to TrainSmith, a fitness application developed by GoalAlly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the TrainSmith mobile application (“App”). By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following types of data:

  • Personal Identification Information: full name (if provided) and email address.
  • Profile and Fitness Data: age, gender, height, weight, workout preferences, training duration, intensity, exercise history, and health-related notes (limitations, contraindications, training preferences).
  • App Usage Data: anonymous data about how users interact with the App (for analytics and troubleshooting).

We do not verify or share your name or email address with third-party services. These data are used only for identifying your account and providing personalized experiences within the App.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Personalize workout plans and recommendations.
  • Analyze and improve app performance and user experience.
  • Provide support and ensure proper App functionality.
  • Manage subscription services and in-app purchases.

We may also use aggregated and anonymized data for analytics and product improvement purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

To ensure stable operation, analytics, and AI-powered functionality, TrainSmith integrates with several third-party services:

  • Firebase – for authentication, analytics, and performance monitoring.
  • Sentry – for error tracking and debugging.
  • OpenAI API and Gemini API – for AI-based workout generation and personalization.
  • RevenueCat – for managing in-app subscriptions and billing.

These providers process certain technical or usage data under their own privacy policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

All user data are securely stored in a cloud-based database with restricted access and encryption. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. Currently, users cannot request full deletion of their personal data; however, we plan to introduce this feature in future updates.

6. Subscriptions and Payments

TrainSmith may offer in-app subscriptions. At present, premium subscriptions do not provide additional features, but future updates may include exclusive or enhanced functionality. All payments are processed securely via the App Store or Google Play billing systems.

7. Children’s Privacy

TrainSmith is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available in the App and will include the updated effective date.
